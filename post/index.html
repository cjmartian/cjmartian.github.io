Bandit Coffee and Matcha

Bandit is a new coffee and matcha shop on North Lamar just past the river. Bandit has a wide selection of drinks and pastries to choose from along with a Lamar-facing patio to enjoy them on. Upon arrival they ask that you either download their app or use a provided kiosk to place your order in an attempt to maintain a contactless transaction which was highly appreciated. Pictured (left to right) is an iced matcha latte, a hot latte, and an iced honey lavender matcha latte accompanied by a chocolate crescent and a blueberry doughnut. »

FoodHeads

FoodHeads is a mostly outdoor sandwich spot on North Guadalupe that features large sandwiches, iced tea, and made-in-kitchen soups. If you ever needed a reason to go get some rainy day comfort food, this is it. FoodHeads features a comfortable, quiet outdoor seating area where you can listen to birds nesting in a nearby birdhouse while you enjoy their food.
